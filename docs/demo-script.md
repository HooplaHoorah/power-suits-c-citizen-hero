# Citizen Hero Demo Script

**Narration** (voiceover or on-screen text):

1. **Hook** (0:00–0:10)  
   - “Meet Citizen Hero: the AI-powered quest forge for everyday heroes.”  
   - Show the Power Suits C logo and tagline: “Turn problems into hero quests.”

2. **Problem statement** (0:10–0:25)  
   - “Kids and solopreneurs want to make a difference, but turning ‘I care’ into ‘I did’ is hard.”  
   - Display photos/illustrations of stray animals, littered parks, lonely seniors.

3. **Introducing the solution** (0:25–0:40)  
   - “Citizen Hero wraps civic engagement in a sci‑fi suit—powered by AI.”  
   - Show the web app landing page with the form.

4. **Walkthrough: Idea capture** (0:40–1:05)  
   - Narration: “Enter a nickname, choose your age range, describe what’s bugging you, and pick the type of help.”  
   - On screen: fill in “JayNova,” select “12‑15,” type “There are lots of stray cats in my neighborhood,” choose “Raise supplies,” and click **Boot Suit**.

5. **Behind the scenes** (1:05–1:20)  
   - “Under the hood, Citizen Hero calls Raindrop’s SmartInference to craft a quest using a carefully designed prompt.”  
   - Overlay: show simplified prompt text and mention the Vultr PostgreSQL DB for storage.

6. **Quest reveal** (1:20–1:50)  
   - On screen: show the Mission Brief with quest name “OPERATION COZY PAWS,” mission summary, steps with SGXP rewards, reflection questions, and safety notes.  
   - Narration: “In seconds, our Suit Brain generates a mission with clear steps and XP rewards—ready to play in the real world.”

7. **Saving to Suit Log** (1:50–2:05)  
   - Click “Save to Suit Log”; show the list of saved quests.  
   - Narration: “Every quest is stored in a Vultr database via Raindrop SmartSQL, so players can revisit their missions.”

8. **Why it matters** (2:05–2:30)  
   - “Citizen Hero turns civic dreams into action. It’s safe, scalable, and fun—perfect for classrooms, youth clubs, and solo changemakers.”

9. **Call to action & future vision** (2:30–2:55)  
   - “Join us in building a generation of everyday heroes.”  
   - Show future visions: a map of ‘Civic City,’ leaderboards, and multiplayer quests.  
   - “We’re just getting started. With Raindrop and Vultr, Citizen Hero will scale to schools, nonprofits, and tournaments.”

10. **Outro** (2:55–3:00)  
    - Show logo and tagline again.  
    - Narration: “Citizen Hero—Forge your quest. Level up your world.”
